Visit Some of the best places in India

The Chittorgarh Fort, also known as Chittod Fort, is one of the largest living forts in India. It holds immense historical significance and has been declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Here are some fascinating details about this iconic fortress.

Geography

1. Chittorgarh is located in the southern part of the state of Rajasthan, approximately 233 km (144.8 mi) from Ajmer

2. The fort stands on a hill 180 m (590.6 ft) high, overlooking the surrounding plains.

3. In 2013, during the 37th session of the World Heritage Committee, Chittorgarh Fort, along with five other forts of Rajasthan, was declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site as part of the group called the Hill Forts of Rajasthan
