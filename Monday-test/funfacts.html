Did You Know?

  • The world's smallest bird is the bee hummingbird.
  • Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood.
  • A group of flamingos is called a "flamboyance".
  • Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still edible.
  • Octopuses have three hearts: two pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body.
  • Bananas are berries, but strawberries aren't.
  • An octopus has nine brains. One central brain controls the nervous system, and there is a small brain in each of its eight arms.
  • Did you know there are nearly 2,000 thunderstorms on Earth every minute?
  • Clouds look white because they are reflecting sunlight from above them.
  • Owls can’t move their eyeballs! Instead they move their neck to look around.
  • Cockroaches can live for a week without their heads!
  • Camel Fact – Its a myth that the camel’s hump stores water! Rather it stores fat that is used for nourishment when the food is scarce.
